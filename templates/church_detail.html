New Hope Evangelistical Church - Kirugu

Faith Into Action

Welcome to New Hope Evangelistical Church — Kirugu, a Christ-centered, Bible-teaching fellowship committed to proclaiming Jesus faithfully and equipping believers to live out their faith daily.

We are devoted to teaching the Word of God clearly, systematically, and practically — helping individuals and families grow strong in truth and grounded in Scripture.

Our Mission in Kirugu

At New Hope Evangelistical Church, we are committed to:

  • Teaching the Bible book by book, chapter by chapter, and verse by verse.
  • Making disciples of Jesus Christ.
  • Raising strong families rooted in biblical truth.
  • Equipping believers to serve their community.
  • Proclaiming the Gospel with boldness and clarity.

We believe faith is not passive — it moves. Faith must be lived.

School Devotions & Chapel

We are actively investing in the next generation through the New Hope Foundation Primary School. We bring biblical teaching, character development, and Gospel clarity directly into the classrooms through:

  • Morning devotions and Midday chapel services.
  • Scripture memory programs.
  • Teacher discipleship and training.

Medical & Social Outreach

Following Christ's example of compassion, we visit the sick and support vulnerable families in Kirugu every week. We provide pastoral care, share Scripture, and offer tangible support through meals and medical welfare.

"I was sick and you visited me..." — Matthew 25:36
