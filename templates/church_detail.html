Our Spiritual Home

Faith Into Action

Welcome to New Hope Evangelistical Church — Kirugu, a Christ-centered fellowship committed to proclaiming Jesus faithfully and equipping believers to live out their faith daily.

We are devoted to teaching the Word of God clearly and systematically—helping individuals and families grow strong in truth. Our mission in Kirugu is built on these pillars:

  • Teaching the Bible verse by verse.
  • Making disciples of Jesus Christ.
  • Raising families rooted in biblical truth.
  • Proclaiming the Gospel with boldness.
