Our Mission

To train young people into Christlikeness and empower communities through holistic ministry involving spiritual growth, education, and sustainable economic projects.

Our Vision

A thriving Africa where every child has access to education, every community is spiritually rooted in Christ, and every family is economically empowered to be self-sustaining.

A Holistic Approach

How We Work

Spiritual Roots

Centered at New Hope Evangelistical Church, we lead crusades and leadership seminars to win souls and build faith foundations.

Child Welfare

Through the New Hope Foundation School, we provide tuition, medical care, and daily meals to vulnerable children in Kirugu.

Economic Growth

Our Fish Farm and Poultry projects aren't just for food; they are training grounds for community members to learn sustainable skills.

The Journey

Founded by Rev. Ronnie and Veronica Baraza, RVM Africa began with a simple but heavy burden: the sight of orphaned children and struggling families in the Nakalanga Parish.

What started as a small gathering under a tree has grown into a multi-faceted mission. Today, we stand as a lighthouse in Kirugu, blending the fire of evangelism with the practical dignity of vocational training and agricultural development.

"We believe a skilled hand is an empowered heart. Transformation must be total—body, mind, and spirit."
