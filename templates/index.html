Ronnie and Vero Ministries Africa is a holistic mission dedicated to the spiritual and physical transformation of communities.

We support vulnerable and orphaned children with tuition, meals, and medical welfare. Our heart is in winning souls through crusades, counseling, and training leaders into Christlikeness.

Beyond spiritual growth, we believe in economic empowerment. We train community skills through Tailoring, Music, Poultry Farming, and Fish Farming.
