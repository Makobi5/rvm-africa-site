Who We Are

Rev. Ronnie & Veronica Baraza

Ronnie and Vero Ministries Africa is a holistic ministry dedicated to the spiritual and physical transformation of communities.

We support vulnerable and orphaned children with tuition, meals, and medical welfare. Our heart is in winning souls through crusades, counseling, and training leaders into Christlikeness.

Beyond spiritual growth, we believe in economic empowerment. We train community members in practical skills like Tailoring, Music, Poultry Farming, and Fish Farming to ensure a self-sustaining future.
