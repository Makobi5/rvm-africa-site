OUR SCHOOL

Nurturing Minds, Building Foundations

Education with Purpose

Academic Excellence & Spiritual Foundation

New Hope Foundation Primary School - Kirugu is more than just a place of learning; it is a center for transformation. We provide quality and affordable education designed to equip learners with skills for a dynamic world.

Guided by our motto, "The Lord is My Shepherd," we believe that true education addresses both the mind and the spirit. We are committed to:

  • Quality Curriculum: Following the National curriculum with added emphasis on critical thinking.
  • Spiritual Growth: Daily devotions and weekly chapel services for all students.
  • Holistic Care: Providing nutritious school meals and medical welfare for orphaned children.

Why New Hope Foundation?

Nutrition Program

We ensure every student receives daily meals, understanding that a hungry child cannot focus on their studies.

Skilled Instructors

Our teachers are mentors who are dedicated to the academic success and moral growth of every learner.

Vulnerable Support

We specialize in enrolling and supporting orphaned and vulnerable children through specialized tuition aid.

A Community Landmark

Located in Kirugu, our facility provides a safe, structured, and inspiring environment for children to discover their God-given potential. We are licensed (MOES ME/P/11545) and recognized as a model school for community-led development.

Vision: To be a model school in providing quality and affordable education that will equip the learners with skills and knowledge to fit in the dynamic world.

Empower a Child’s Future

Your partnership allows us to continue providing tuition support and meals to children who need it most. Join us in raising the next generation of Kirugu's leaders.
