OUR STORY & VISION

Our Mission

To train young people into Christlikeness and empower communities through holistic ministry involving spiritual growth, education, and sustainable economic projects.

Our Vision

A thriving Africa where every child has access to education, every community is spiritually rooted in Christ, and every family is economically empowered to be self-sustaining.

The Journey

RVM Africa started in Kirugu with a small group of believers and a big vision. We saw that orphaned children needed more than just a church—they needed a future. This led to the creation of the New Hope Foundation School and our agricultural projects.

Today, our Kirugu Fish Farm and Poultry projects serve as a model for how a ministry can provide for itself and train its people in vocational skills like tailoring and music.
