The Founders

Rev. Ronnie & Veronica Baraza

"Our mission was born from a calling to see Africa transformed by the power of the Gospel and the dignity of self-reliance."

Rev. Ronnie and Veronica Baraza have dedicated their lives to ministry in Uganda. Their journey began with a vision to reach the most vulnerable, not just with words, but with a holistic hand of support.

Rev. Ronnie, known for his evangelistic fire and leadership in New Hope Evangelistical Church, works alongside Veronica to manage their various outreaches—from the poultry farms that feed the community to the school that educates the next generation of leaders.
